# AI Native

## What is an AI Native Software Development Team?

An AI-native software development team is not one that *uses* AI tools — it is one that *cannot function without them*. AI is not bolted on as a feature or productivity booster; it is woven into the fabric of how the team thinks, builds, ships, and operates. Remove the AI, and the team stops working.

This is the fundamental line between **AI-enhanced** and **AI-native**: one has driver assist, the other is self-driving.

> [!quote] **Ethan Mollick** — *Wharton, One Useful Thing*

## The Defining Principle

> **Your job is to automate your job.**

Build the systems, governance, and skills that reduce repetitive work and increase leverage across the organization. The human layer shifts toward architecture, intent-setting, judgment, and orchestration — not execution.

The new agentic engineer blends software development expertise with AI orchestration: coordinating agents to do the heavy lifting, while focusing energy on architecture, integration, and intent.

---

## Why It Matters

Teams are not necessarily getting smaller — but they are producing dramatically more. 2–5x output is becoming the baseline expectation. 65% of developers expect their roles to be redefined by 2026, moving from routine coding toward architecture, integration, and AI-enabled decision-making.

The competitive unit is no longer the team. It is the **team × its agents.**
