# Andrej Karpathy

AI researcher and educator. Former Director of AI at Tesla and founding member of OpenAI. Author of the [[LLM Wiki Pattern]] — the foundational design pattern behind this vault.

## Contributions to This Knowledge Base

- Described the LLM Wiki Pattern: LLMs incrementally building and maintaining a persistent wiki rather than re-deriving knowledge via RAG on every query
- Framed the three-layer architecture: raw sources → wiki → schema (CLAUDE.md / AGENTS.md)
- Connected the pattern to Vannevar Bush's Memex (1945) as its intellectual ancestor
