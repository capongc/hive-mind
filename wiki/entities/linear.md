# Linear

Project management tool and cited [[AI-Native Team]] exemplar. Notable for treating agents as first-class team members — assigning tasks to agents the same way tasks are assigned to humans. This framing (agents as teammates, not tools) is central to the [[AI-Native SDLC]] philosophy. Referenced in [[AI Native]] alongside [[Ramp]], [[OpenAI]], and [[swyx]].
