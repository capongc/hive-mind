# Anthropic

AI safety company and creator of the Claude model family. Publisher of the Building Effective AI Agents whitepaper — a comprehensive guide to production agent architecture patterns.

## Key Research / Publications

- **Building Effective AI Agents** — whitepaper covering single-agent, hierarchical, collaborative, sequential, parallel, and evaluator-optimizer patterns with a decision framework for matching patterns to use cases
- Internal research: multi-agent systems outperform single-agent by **90.2%** on complex tasks requiring multiple simultaneous directions
- [[Claude Code]] — agentic coding CLI; cited by [[Ramp]] as a baseline performance expectation

## Models

- Claude Sonnet — recommended for reasoning-heavy tasks like the [[Engineering Lead Role]]
- Claude family spans from high-capability reasoning models to fast, lightweight models for high-volume tasks
