# Agentuity

AI company credited with coining the "driver-assist vs. self-driving" framing for distinguishing AI-enhanced teams from [[AI-Native Team]]s. The framing draws a hard line: an AI-native team cannot function without AI (self-driving), while an AI-enhanced team merely uses AI as an aid (driver assist). Referenced in the [[AI Native]] source.
