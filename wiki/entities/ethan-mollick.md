# Ethan Mollick

AI researcher and educator who advocates treating AI as a genuine colleague — assigning it a role, giving it context, and holding it responsible for outcomes. This perspective directly supports the [[AI-Native Team]] model where agents are teammates, not utilities. Referenced in the [[AI Native]] source alongside [[swyx]] and [[Agentuity]].
