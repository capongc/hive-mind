# Cory Hymel

Engineer and researcher at [[Crowdbotics]]. Author of the white paper *"The AI-Native Software Development Lifecycle: A Theoretical and Practical New Methodology"*, in which he proposed the [[V-Bounce Model]].

## Contributions

- Proposed and named the [[V-Bounce Model]] for AI-native software development
- Framed the shift from human-as-creator to human-as-verifier in AI-assisted development
