## Summary

Describes the Engineering Lead agent role — the visionary architect of a virtual AI team that translates natural language requirements into detailed design specifications and delegates discrete tasks to specialized developer agents.

## Key Claims

- The Engineering Lead is the primary interface for user interaction and overall system management
- It decides when to reuse existing code vs. trigger new implementations
- After finalizing design, it delegates to specialized agents (backend, frontend, etc.)
- Powered by reasoning-heavy models like Claude 3.5 Sonnet due to planning complexity
