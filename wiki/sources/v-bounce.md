# V-Bounce

**Source:** raw/assets/v-bounce.md
**Date ingested:** 2026-04-18
**Type:** notes

## Summary

Deep dive on the V-Bounce model — a reimagining of the traditional V-model in systems engineering, adapted for AI-primary implementation. Proposed by [[Cory Hymel]] of [[Crowdbotics]] in the white paper *"The AI-Native Software Development Lifecycle: A Theoretical and Practical New Methodology"*.

## Key Claims

- The "bounce" refers to near-instantaneous time at the bottom of the V (the coding phase) — AI generates code so fast teams bounce from design straight to validation
- Humans operate at the top of the V: high-level requirements and final verification/validation
- AI generates test cases in real-time as natural language requirements are input — every requirement is testable the moment it's conceived
- Simultaneous req/test creation yields an inherent traceability matrix, simplifying change management
- V-Bounce directly replaces the traditional 2-week sprint cycle
