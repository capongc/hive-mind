# AI-Native Team

A software development team that *cannot function without AI* — not one that merely uses AI tools for productivity. The defining test: remove the AI, and the team stops working entirely.

## The Hard Line

The distinction between AI-enhanced and AI-native:
- **AI-enhanced:** driver assist — human is in control, AI helps around the edges
- **AI-native:** self-driving — AI is at the wheel, humans guide direction

Source: [[Agentuity]]

## Core Principle

> Your job is to automate your job.

Humans shift from execution to architecture, intent-setting, judgment, and orchestration. The new agentic engineer blends software development expertise with AI orchestration — coordinating the [[Agent Swarm]] while focusing energy on architecture, integration, and intent.

## The Competitive Unit

The competitive unit is no longer the team. It is the **team × its agents**.

- 2–5x output is the baseline expectation
- 65% of developers expect their roles to be redefined by 2026
- Some teams (e.g. Atlassian via [[OpenAI]]) have engineers writing zero lines of code
